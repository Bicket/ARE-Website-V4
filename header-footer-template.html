About ARE

The premier battlefield community for team-oriented gamers on PlayStation 4! Well respected in the battlefield community, ARE is committed to being the place for tactical and well-rounded players that value strategy, communication, coordination, and collaboration with others. ARE provides an opportunity for these players to enjoy themselves, play battlefield the way it was meant to be played, and fight alongside other like-minded individuals in an organized environment.
