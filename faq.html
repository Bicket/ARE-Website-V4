Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the team?

To join ARE you must first submit an application on our website. We encourage you to take a look around our site and read about the team before applying! Once you’ve submitted an application you will be directed to our Discord server where you will be able to get in touch directly with ARE members and play with Ambassadors!

Can I speak with someone in ARE directly?

Our Recruiters work exclusively with visitors and those looking to apply. You can speak with a recruiter 24/7 in our Visitor’s channel (“Visitors Lobby”) on Discord. Learn more about Discord below!

What is Discord?

ARE is the premier battlefield community for team-oriented gamers on PlayStation 4. We strive for quality over quantity in all that we do, offering only the best in resources and opportunities for our team! Because of this we decide to use Discord as our primary communication platform in the team. If you are familiar with Skype or Teamspeak you will feel right at home here on Discord. What we use Discord for:
  1. Team announcements. Forget having to scroll through pages of forums... Get smart notifications on your phone, computer, or web browser keeping you updated with the latest happenings in ARE.
  2. Find people to squad up. It takes an average of 2 minutes for someone to respond back to your request for teammates! Never enter the battlefield alone again!
  3. Cross-party communication. It is not uncommon for there to be multiple ARE PSN Parties up at one time. We use Discord to communicate between these parties through the use of text/voice channels.
  4. Competitive matches! Setting up competitive battles are a breeze. View the current battle channel for rules, match updates, and practice times. You can even join in on the conversation as we talk strats!
  5. Mobile App. Browser compatible. Sync across multiple devices. Great interface.

What is a “Prospect”?

A Prospect is someone who has applied and is in the recruitment process to join ARE but is not a member of ARE. During this “prospect phase” players are given the opportunity to play with us and get a real taste of what it's like to play as part of a team. We also use this time as an opportunity to get to know you as a PLAYER and as a PERSON.

Is ARE looking for staff?

Staff opportunities are available within ARE after the application process has been completed. Available positions are listed with job information. You must have been a member of ARE for a minimum of 60 days before being considered for a staff position.

Do you guys fight in competitive battles? If so, how do I get involved?

Yes!! We hold practices often and participate in at least one clan battle every month, if not more. These battles are highly competitive and involve a lot of organizing and planning - even still we really enjoy doing them! Anyone in ARE has the opportunity to participate in competitive battles. That being said there are 3 ways that we go about choosing the roster:
  1. Most common. First come, first serve. For example: A 10v10 match is announced and the first 10 to sign up get to participate.
  2. Common. Attendance at practice lands you a spot on the roster. For example: A 20v20 match is announced along with a list of practices leading up to the match. Out of those who attend the practices 20 people are chosen to play in the match.
  3. Occasionally. Roster is chosen by the event organizer. For example: A 5v5 match is announced and the event organizer chooses who participates.
We are currently involved with or have been a part of tournaments, leagues, and events through the following organizations:
eSports Battlefield (ESB) , Electronic Sports League (ESL), Fragged Nation (FN), Glory 4 Gamers (G4G), and others.

What happens to me if I become busy for a period of time?

When you become a member, you’re considered family from that day forward. If, however, you do need to be offline for an extended period of time we have a system to accommodate this. We are real people with real lives, just like yourself. We understand life and we understand priorities! If you plan to be “inactive” for a while please:
  1. Let us know us know ahead of time! This is just courteous on your end, and doesn’t leave us wondering if everything is okay!
  2. If you’re able to, we would encourage you to still stay in touch through our communication platform, Discord, during your absence.
  3. When you are able to return back to playing you will pick up where you left off...with familiar faces welcoming you back, new faces to greet you, and a welcome team that will always be there.

What platform does ARE operate on?

We are a PlayStation community founded on PS3, now predominantly based on PS4, with goals to expand and grow wherever life takes us. We currently support PlayStation 4. We have plans to open a small PC group in 2017. We have no plans to expand into the XBOX consoles.

How will I be contacted regarding announcements?

We use Discord, and occasionally email, to notify our members of anything community related. Being active on our communication outlets is fantastic, but it is even more important that you are aware of any announcements coming through Discord and email (when an email goes out it will also be announced on Discord)!

Do you support games other than just Battlefield? What are your plans for the future?

ARE officially supports one primary game, and occasionally one secondary game as well. This is to stay true to our commitment of Quality over Quantity. We have quite a few “unofficially supported” games that ARE members will play in their downtime or during gamenights. However, we are first and foremost a Battlefield community, and that is where you will find us.
Official Support:
  • Primary:   Battlefield 4/battlefield 1
  • Secondary:   Rainbow Six Siege
We are eagerly anticipating the launch of Battlefield 1!
